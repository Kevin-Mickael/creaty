Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with Creaty.

1. Why should I hire an agency instead of doing it myself?
Social media and digital strategy require more than just posting a photo. It involves graphic design, copywriting, data analysis, and staying updated with ever-changing algorithms. By hiring Creaty, you gain a professional team and save hours of work, allowing you to focus entirely on running your business while we ensure your brand stays ahead of the competition.
2. How long does it take to see real results?
Digital growth is a marathon, not a sprint. While you will see an immediate improvement in your brand's look and feel, significant results in engagement and conversions typically take 3 to 6 months of consistent strategy. We provide monthly reports so you can track the progress and see the value we build over time.
3. Will I own my accounts and digital assets?
Absolutely. This is one of our core values. Unlike some agencies, we ensure that you remain the primary owner of your social media profiles, domain names, and professional emails. We are here to manage them for you, but they always belong to you. Your success and your assets stay in your hands.
4. Do you offer custom packages for small businesses?
Yes. We understand that every business is at a different stage. While we have standard service tiers, we can tailor our "Digital & Social Media" solutions to fit your specific goals and budget. Our aim is to build a foundation that grows as your revenue grows.
5. What kind of content will you create for my brand?
We create a mix of high-quality graphics, professional videos (Reels/TikToks), and compelling captions tailored to your brand’s voice. Everything is planned in advance through a content calendar that you get to review and approve, so there are never any surprises.
6. How do we communicate and stay in touch?
Transparency is key for us. We typically set up a dedicated communication channel (like WhatsApp or Email) and hold monthly strategy meetings to review Performance Reports. You will always have a clear view of what we are working on and the results we are achieving together.
